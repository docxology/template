# Citation Style Guide

These are **soft** guidelines for citation formatting. Apply them
consistently; flag deviations as suggestions.

---

## Default Style

Use **APA 7th edition** as the default citation style unless the target venue
specifies otherwise. Venue-specific overrides take precedence.

---

## In-Text Citations

- Author–date format: `(Smith & Jones, 2021)` or `Smith and Jones (2021)`.
- Three or more authors: `(Smith et al., 2021)` after first occurrence.
- Multiple citations in one parenthetical: alphabetical by first author,
  separated by semicolons: `(Brown, 2019; Smith & Jones, 2021)`.
- Direct quotations: include page number: `(Smith & Jones, 2021, p. 42)`.

---

## Reference List Formatting

- Alphabetical by first author's surname.
- Hanging indent (0.5 in / 1.27 cm).
- DOI as a hyperlink when available: `https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx`.
- For preprints, include the repository and ID: `arXiv:2301.00001`.

### Journal Article

```
Smith, A. B., & Jones, C. D. (2021). Title of the article. *Journal Name*,
  *12*(3), 45–67. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx
```

### Conference Paper

```
Smith, A. B. (2022). Title of the paper. In *Proceedings of the Conference
  Name* (pp. 100–110). Publisher. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx
```

### Book

```
Jones, C. D. (2020). *Title of the book* (2nd ed.). Publisher.
```

### Preprint

```
Smith, A. B., & Jones, C. D. (2023). *Title of the preprint*.
  arXiv:2301.00001. https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.00001
```

---

## Software and Datasets

- Cite software with version, repository URL, and DOI if available.
- Cite datasets with their official reference; do not substitute a paper
  about the dataset if a dataset-specific DOI exists.

---

## Self-Citation

- Self-citations are acceptable when genuinely necessary for context.
- Do not pad the reference list with self-citations that are not cited in
  the text.

---

## Verification

- Every citation should be verifiable against a DOI, URL, or ISBN.
- Flag any citation without a verifiable identifier for manual review.
