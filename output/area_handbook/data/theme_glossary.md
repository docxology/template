## Theme glossary

| Theme | Label | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| `landscape` | Landscape | Geography, natural features, and built form at regional scale. |
| `communities` | Communities | Neighborhoods, demographics, civic organizations, and social infrastructure. |
| `economy` | Economy | Employment, sectors, wages, and fiscal flows. |
| `infrastructure` | Infrastructure | Transport, utilities, broadband, and maintenance backlogs. |
| `environment` | Environment | Air and water quality, open space, hazards, and climate exposure. |
| `governance` | Governance | Jurisdictions, service delivery, and interlocal agreements. |
| `risks` | Risks | Cross-cutting vulnerabilities and compounding shocks. |
| `recommendations` | Recommendations | Prioritized actions and stewardship of the living handbook. |
