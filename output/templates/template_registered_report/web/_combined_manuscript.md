# Abstract

This exemplar demonstrates preregistration locking, deviation reporting, and confirmatory-versus-exploratory claim boundaries.



---



# Methods

The method freezes a registration payload, validates required sections, records registered-report stage and ethics-review metadata, and compares executed analyses against registered outcomes. The project artifact generator exports the frozen registration, adherence report, deviation ledger, sensitivity findings, and review packet while keeping confirmatory outcomes, exploratory outcomes, and sensitivity-analysis decisions separate.



---



# Results

Results are interpreted only after the deviation ledger classifies any departures from the registered plan. The generated fixture review packet preserves the registration hash, confirms the primary outcome as confirmatory, and treats the example secondary endpoint and model change as exploratory because each has a rationale-bearing deviation.
