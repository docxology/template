**Remembrances**

**OBITUARIES.** `15_obituaries` uses dignified, impersonal templates. Deceased individuals are not named; survivors are not listed. Real obituaries require fact-checking against family wishes; this page models tone and measure only.

Service traditions—visitation hours, memorial donations, religious officiants—appear as generic phrases. Military honors and cultural rites differ widely; writers should localize.

Community volunteers and educators receive boilerplate praise. Achievements are summarized without dates that would stale quickly. Photographs would sit beside text with cropped portraits; figure placement rules mirror other sections.

Cause-of-death ethics: omit unless families consent. This stub avoids causes entirely. Newspapers follow stylebooks; your project should too when live.

Legacy statements mention gardens planted, students mentored, music shared. Emotional bandwidth in obits is high; line breaks should feel respectful, not cramped.

Legal notices sometimes adjoin obits; spacing and rule lines help separation. `snippets.rule_line` can draw thin rules when needed.

The section closes quietly. Classifieds—commercial lines—finish the core sixteen on the next folio.

**Depth notes.** Obituary writers interview families under grief stress; empathy and accuracy both matter. Style guides specify ordering of life facts—birth, death, education, career, survivors. This stub omits facts entirely.

Death notices paid by funeral homes differ editorially from staff-written obits. Revenue mixes with sensitivity. Ethical walls should separate sales from news—harder in small markets.

Memorial services during pandemics moved online; archived streams become historical records. Link rot threatens; institutions should preserve copies.

Notable figures warrant long obits filed in advance (“advances”) updated on publication. News alerts trigger fast edits. Template PDFs regenerate from git state—treat main branch as publication truth.

Donation suggestions (“in lieu of flowers”) channel grief toward causes. Verify charity tax status before print. Synthetic text here names no charities.

Cultural variations in mourning periods and public expression affect coverage. Editors localize sensitivity reads.

Obits pass: `15_obituaries` exits on a soft period. `16_classifieds.md` closes the numeric run with utilitarian lines.
