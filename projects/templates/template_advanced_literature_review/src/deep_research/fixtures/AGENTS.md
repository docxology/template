# Recorded Replay Fixtures — Agent Directives

Keep replay inputs deterministic, transparent, and safe for public CI. Citation
metadata must resolve to real primary sources. Do not add invented titles,
quotes, offsets, or `example.org` placeholders. The report body is synthetic
demonstration content and must remain labelled as such in `raw.fixture_scope`.
Provider, job, and trace fields must identify curation and must never imply a
paid provider produced the fixture.
