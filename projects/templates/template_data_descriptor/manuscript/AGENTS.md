# manuscript - AGENTS.md

Keep prose claims tied to `data/example_descriptor.json` and the tested
`src/data_descriptor/` package (`descriptor.py`, `figures.py`, `verification.py`).
Do not transcribe volatile numbers into prose; cite the artifact that produces
them.

Figures are embedded with `![caption](figures/<name>.png){#fig:<name>}` and must
correspond to real files under `figures/` generated by
`scripts/generate_figures.py`. A config-only figure registry entry does not
render — every figure needs a real embed block. Claims stay bound to dataset
structure, schema, provenance, license state, byte-level integrity, and
release-readiness evidence; never to empirical effects in the synthetic data.
