Appendix B — Notation and Symbols

Reference appendix · Symbol glossary for the worked models.

This appendix lists the symbols used by the worked formalisms in src/textbook/models.py. Keep it in sync with the parameter tables ({#tbl:...}) in the chapters: every symbol that appears in an equation {#eq:...} should have a row here.

Conventions

  • Scalars are italic lowercase (e.g. r, t); sets and spaces are uppercase.
  • Subscript 0 denotes an initial value (e.g. N₀ at t = 0).
  • Units are stated in SI / metric unless a chapter declares otherwise.
